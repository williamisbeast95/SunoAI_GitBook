# Vocal Design

## Overview
Vocal design focuses on shaping how the voice sounds, feels, and delivers emotion.

## Key Elements

- tone (soft, aggressive, emotional)
- delivery (rap, melodic, spoken)
- effects (reverb, distortion, layering)

## Tips

- define emotion clearly
- match vocal style to genre
- avoid conflicting instructions

## Goal

Create vocals that feel intentional and aligned with the track.
