# Iteration Strategies

## Overview
Iteration is the core of improving results. Instead of relying on a single output, creators refine through multiple passes.

## Core Strategy

- identify what works
- isolate what needs improvement
- adjust prompts or structure
- regenerate or extend

## Tips

- track changes between versions
- avoid making too many changes at once
- focus on improving one aspect per iteration

## Goal

Turn rough outputs into polished tracks through controlled refinement.
